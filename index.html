FAQ

Before you grab the mic.

What are your hours?

We're open Monday–Friday from 6:00 PM to 2:00 AM, and Saturday–Sunday from 3:00 PM to 2:00 AM. The kitchen stays open late — last orders at 1:30 AM.

How big are your private rooms?

We offer private rooms of various sizes, accommodating small gatherings all the way up to private parties of 50 people. Our dining area can also be converted into a VIP karaoke room upon request.

Is Moxie a restaurant too?

Yes — we are more than just a karaoke venue. We are a Japanese restaurant specializing in sushi and grills, with a full food and drinks menu.

How does the Happy Hour Special work?

It's $30 per person for the entire duration. This charge includes complimentary food and drinks valued up to $30 from our menu. Available Monday–Friday 6 PM–8 PM and Saturday–Sunday 3 PM–7 PM.

Do I have to drink to have a good time?

Absolutely not. We cater to all preferences — whether you're enjoying a drink or embracing the new wave of sober culture. It's a welcoming and balanced environment for everyone.

Is Moxie family friendly?

Yes. Moxie is an ideal spot for family gatherings, where guests of all ages can enjoy an unforgettable experience.

Can I order to-go?

Yes — we're on DoorDash, Uber Eats, and GrubHub for delivery and pickup.

How do I make a reservation?

Book directly through our Reservations page — you can secure your room online in just a few clicks. You can also call us at 626-525-8889 or email info@moxiekaraoke.com. Weekends book up fast — reserve early!
